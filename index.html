Contrarian Index
The Contrarian Index measures how often each member disagrees with group consensus. For every book, we calculate how far each member's score deviates from the club average. These absolute deviations are averaged across all books rated — giving one contrarian score per member. Near 0 = always agrees with the group  ·  Higher = more of an outlier. Directional bias shows whether a member tends to rate above or below average.
